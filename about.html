About Stellar

Learn about our mission, team, and the technology behind our constellation detection platform

The Story

This project leverages the rich information about constellations and stars to develop a constellation detection system. By using star positions, brightness, and constellation boundaries, the system can identify constellations in the night sky or in images. The guide's detailed star data, constellation diagrams, and helpful alignments provide essential reference points for accurate detection..

Additionally, the project benefits from the mythology and cultural stories behind constellations, which can be used to create engaging educational content alongside the detection functionality.

Overall, this project combines astronomical data and cultural knowledge to build a tool that helps users explore and learn about the night sky through constellation detection.

Our Mission

To democratize astronomy by making constellation identification accessible to everyone, regardless of their prior knowledge or equipment. We strive to combine advanced technology with educational content to create an engaging platform that inspires curiosity about the cosmos.

Our Vision

A world where anyone can look up at the night sky and understand what they're seeing. We envision Stellar becoming the go-to platform for constellation identification and astronomical learning, bridging the gap between casual observation and scientific understanding.

Our Technology

The innovative technologies powering our constellation detection platform

Machine Learning

Our advanced machine learning algorithms are trained on thousands of astronomical images to accurately identify star patterns and constellations in user-uploaded photos.

Computer Vision

We use state-of-the-art computer vision techniques to process and analyze night sky images, detecting stars and their relative positions with high precision.

Astronomical Database

Our comprehensive database contains detailed information about all 88 officially recognized constellations, including star maps, mythology, and scientific data.
